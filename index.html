01 · Glossary

Speak the Language

Tap any term to learn what it means.

💰 CHARGE TYPES

🏗️
THC
Terminal Handling Charge
▼
The base fee for handling a container at the terminal. Covers receiving it from the vessel and placing it in the yard — the "service fee" for using the port.

Two types: G/W Tariff (local import, higher rate) and T/S Tariff (transhipment, lower rate to attract transit volume).
💵
ORC
Operational Recovery Charge
▼
AED 367 ($100) per container. Charged from the date the vessel berths (not when discharged). On top of THC. Same for all tiers. Effective 15 Mar 2026 — circular has been released. Subject to VAT for Tier I & II.
🚛
TLUC
Truck Loading / Unloading Charge
▼
Fee for lifting a container on/off a truck at the terminal. The "last-mile handling" within the port.

Rate: AED 380 per container for G/W cargo (increased from AED 320). Effective 15 Mar 2026.
🏗️
Lo-Lo
Lift-on / Lift-off
▼
Crane operation lifting a container on or off a vessel. In some products (ADP/DPW routes), only 50% is charged because KCT handles just one leg.
🛣️
Corridor Fee
Bonded Movement Fee
▼
Charge for trucking a container overland between terminals under customs bond. Covers truck, driver, and bond paperwork. Varies by route: $544/box to $750/TEU. Subject to VAT.
❄️
Reefer
Refrigerated Container Charge
▼
AED 300 per container (increased from AED 155). Dangerous Goods add $50/TEU + $150 genset hire. Effective 15 Mar 2026.
📦
Storage
Yard Storage / Demurrage
▼
Daily fee for keeping a container in the yard. Free days: CMA 15d, MSC 20d (T/S only). MSC gets 30% discount after free days. Tier II: no free days.

🏢 BUSINESS TERMS

🏭
GT · ML · KCT · SCT
Key Organisations
▼
GT (Gulftainer) — Terminal operator. Issues most invoices.
ML (Momentum Logistics) — Handles bonded truck movements. Bills GT or consignee.
KCT (Khorfakkan Container Terminal) — Primary terminal in this guide.
SCT (Sharjah Container Terminal) — Sister terminal for Product 2.
📊
G/W · T/S · TEU · DG · TOS
Technical Terms
▼
G/W Tariff — Gateway rate for local imports (higher).
T/S Tariff — Transhipment rate, ship-to-ship (lower).
TEU — Twenty-Foot Equivalent Unit. 20ft = 1 TEU, 40ft = 2 TEU.
DG — Dangerous Goods. Attracts surcharges.
TOS — Terminal Operating System. Each container mapped to a product here.
👤
Consignee · Shipping Line
Key Roles
▼
Consignee — Person/company receiving cargo. In Product 5, billed directly by ML.
Shipping Line — Companies like CMA, MSC, Maersk. Primary billing customer.
Box Operator — Container lessors. Billing shifted to shipping lines from 15 Mar 2026.

The Two-Invoice Cycle

Every container generates exactly two invoices:

Invoice #1

Vessel Completion

  • THC — Terminal Handling
  • ORC — Operational Recovery
  • + Corridor & Reefer (Product 2 only)
⚡ Vessel procedure completes
Invoice #2

Container Departure

  • TLUC — Truck loading
  • Storage — Yard time
  • Reefer — Refrigeration
  • Corridor — Bonded transport
⚡ Container leaves KCT
💡
Exceptions: Product 2 bills both legs on Invoice #1. Product 5's Invoice #2 goes from ML → Consignee.
03 · Customer Tiers

Who Pays What

Every shipping line falls into one of three tiers.

Contracted

ESL · Fermion
✓ Individually negotiated rates
✓ Custom storage terms per contract
✓ Gate fee waived

Tier I — Major Lines

CMA · MSC · Maersk · COSCO · EGL · PIL · SLSD · ONE · OOCL · HPGL
✓ Tariff-shared rates (lower than published)
✓ CMA: 15 free days / MSC: 20 free days (T/S)
✓ MSC: 30% storage discount after free days
✓ VAT on ORC & Corridor · Valid to 31 Mar 2026

Tier II — Others

All other shipping lines
✓ Tariff-shared rates (higher tier)
✗ No free storage days
✓ VAT on ORC & Corridor · Valid to 31 Mar 2026
